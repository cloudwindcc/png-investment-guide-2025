🔥 Latest August 2025 Policies

⚖️

Mining Act New Regulations

Effective Immediately
  • ✅ State can acquire 30% equity (deferred payment)
  • ✅ Royalty fees: 5% with equity, 10% without equity
  • ❌ Placer mining restricted to PNG citizens only
  • 💰 20% environmental security bond required
🏭

Special Economic Zone Benefits

Super Benefits
  • 🎯 Zero corporate income tax for first 10 years
  • 🎯 Full tariff exemption on imported equipment
  • 🎯 100% first-year accelerated depreciation
  • 🎯 Foreign exchange control exemption
⚠️ New Foreign Exchange Control Directive (Effective October 2025): Orders over 3 million kina require complete due diligence. Essential goods (food, medicine, infrastructure) processed first, non-essentials (dividend remittance) processed last.
